The Gathering

What to Expect

Join Roberta and Luis at their home in Upper Montclair — just 12 miles west of New York City — for a day of food, laughter, lawn games, and memories.

🏡

The Setting

410 Grove Street, Upper Montclair, NJ. A big front porch, backyard patio, and a grill — the perfect backdrop for a summer reunion. Rain or shine.

🍖

Food & Drink

Please arrive anytime after 10 AM. Food and drinks flowing all day. We will have burgers and dogs on the grill as well as fish, vegan burgers and grilled veggies. We will have all the classic salads and sides with a variey of pies for dessert. We will aim for Family Circle at 1 PM !

💛

Suggested Contribution

In past years we've asked family members to bring a dish to pass. This year we're doing it differently — food and drinks are fully covered! We simply suggest a contribution of $25 per person to help offset costs. No obligation, and no one will be turned away. We just want everyone there!

🎯

Lawn Games

Horseshoes, bocce, and croquet on the back lawn. Bring your competitive spirit — or just your lawn chair.

✈️

Getting Here

Easy access from Newark Airport and Newark Penn Station (Amtrak). We're happy to help arrange pickups. Parking available on-site and nearby.

📅

The Weekend

Optional before and after events are being planned — a Friday dinner and Sunday Paterson walking tour. Check the Events section and let us know your interest!

⚽

World Cup Note

The FIFA World Cup begins June 13 at nearby MetLife Stadium. Book lodging early — prices and availability will tighten significantly as that date approaches.
